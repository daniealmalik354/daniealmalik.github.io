About Me

I am currently a marine science master’s student at Diponegoro University. I love the sea with nature and the beauty of life in it. My love for marine life made me want to pursue a career as a marine researcher and learn about the developments in this field. Understanding how marine ecology works can create a mosaic of happiness and gratitude within me. In addition, I worked a lot to help identify and detect population genetics for Indonesian sharks and rays. I have experience using DNA barcoding techniques to reveal the species of shark fins and tracing the genetic populations of sharks and rays using mtDNA and microsatellite DNA methods. In addition, my thesis focuses on using environmental DNA (eDNA) to reveal fish diversity. I am focusing not only on the molecular technique but also on the bioinformatics analysis of the data.
